Decades of Expertise in Energy Industry

Founded in March 1994, UNION is one of the earliest private sector companies in southern China that specialized in devising power generation solutions for a variety of clients. Over the decades, we have been dealing in and providing support for a considerable amount of diesel/HFO generators on land and vessels. We possess rich experiences in designing end-to-end energy solutions: from planning to purchasing, installation, testing, commissioning; as well as maintenance and supplying spare parts.

Pioneer of Green Energy Development

As the cost of fossil fuel energy started to surge and the awareness of environmental protection continued to grow, UNION have begun seeking for alternatives since the trend emerged. In the late 2000s, we were among the first practitioners of biomass energy, accumulating numerous technological innovations and patents throughout our research. Compared to UNION’s other mature projects, our biomass proposition failed to scale because the concept and application was way ahead of time. Nonetheless, we gained deeper insights of how renewable energy business models can be successful, especially within an east or southeast Asia context.

We are always looking for innovative implementations of green energy so that it is more feasible and profitable. Fortunately, as various renewable energy technologies are becoming affordable and accessible, our ambition can be realized. We often find it more environmentally friendly and cost-effective to plug green energy components into traditional power generation-consumption pipeline. Therefore, green energy is likely to be an essential part of future power plants; both existing power generators and users will be able to benefit from the transition to renewable energy. We dedicate ourselves to the march towards new energy by integrating green energy into current energy grid smartly and gradually.

Rapid & Professional Services Across Asia

Over the years, UNION’s business network extends far beyond the borders of China. We have participated in the organization and construction of multiple power plants in Cambodia, Malaysia, etc. We are able to deploy our experienced staff overseas and set up the supply chain in a short amount of time.
